Letters from Niket Desai

Niket,

I've said it once, and I'll say it again: Cancer ain't got shit on Niket Desai. I've thought of you as a fighter for as long as I've known you. I just had to think back to your first semester in Delta Chi. I mean, pushing Will, a man twice your size, into a vending machine during your first week? Climbing under the Bago to fix the dislodged battery that was leaking acid? (PS-- as long as your in the hospital, ask the doctors if they notice any lingering adverse effects from that battery acid). Eating some of the fruity pebbles to get through I-week challenges more quickly? You are one crazy and brave dude. And you're an inspirational one; I'll tell you more about it in person, but you've had a big role in motivating me to make a huge upcoming life-change, which I need to thank you for.

I really hope that this seemingly terrible dot connects somewhere amazing when you look back upon it. And since it's Niket Desai's dot, I bet it will. Stay hungry, stay foolish, right?

Love you bud-- and I'll see you soon.

Brian Zimring

Wishing you a quick recovery. Looking forward to more great times ahead. Your friend, Dylan

Dylan Storm
